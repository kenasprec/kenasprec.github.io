Hi! My name is Kendal.

I am currently a fourth year undergraduate studying Cognitive Science at the University of California, Berkeley. With the knowledge I learn from my studies, I hope to create beautiful products that serve the wants and needs of the people using the human-centered design approach.

As time progressed, I found myself leaning more towards the more creative side of the field while avoiding the actual programming. Eventually, at the summer of 2018, I decided to take a set of online Coursera classes labeled User Interface Design Specialization. I instantly fell in love.

When I'm not spending time interacting with different prototypes, I spend time interacting with people. Find me playing different kinds of sports, sampling the local delicacies, or simply taking a seat and starting a pleasant conversation.
