Hi! My name is Kendal.

I am a UX Researcher who always prioritizes the needs of the user.

As a recent graduate of the University of California, Berkeley with a B.A. in Cognitive Science, I have spent the past four years studying the human mind and its processes. Through the many interdisciplinary studies that form Cognitive Science, I learned a great deal about technology, people, and how people experience technology. I am fascinated by the wide range of experiences that different users have with the same interface, and I love finding the changes that could be made in order to improve the user's experience overall.

When I'm not spending time learning about a user's problems and discovering usability issues, I enjoy spending my time interacting with people. Find me attending a wide variety of events, playing different kinds of games, or simply taking a seat and starting a pleasant conversation.
